Last updated: 28 June 2026

⚠️ This is a starting template, not legal advice. Review and adapt it (company name, jurisdiction, data processors) — and have it checked by a professional — before relying on it in production.

1. Who we are

“Everything You Need” (“we”, “us”) operates this website. For any privacy question, contact us at connect@akshaykotish.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data — your email address and a securely hashed password when you sign up.
  • Session data — a session cookie that keeps you signed in.
  • Usage data — basic, non-identifying information about how pages are used, to improve the service.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and authenticate you.
  • To provide access to gated downloads and features.
  • To protect the service against abuse (e.g. rate limiting and brute-force protection).

4. Cookies

We use a strictly necessary session cookie to keep you logged in. Any non-essential cookies are only set with your consent — see the cookie banner. You can change your choice anytime by clearing this site's cookies.

5. How we protect your data

Passwords are stored using PBKDF2 hashing with per-user salts. Cookies are marked secure on non-local connections. We apply rate limiting and account lockout to defend against brute-force attacks.

6. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers strictly necessary to operate the service, or where required by law.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email connect@akshaykotish.com.

8. Data retention

We keep account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact

Questions? Reach us at connect@akshaykotish.com.
